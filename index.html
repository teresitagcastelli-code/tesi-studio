Process

Slow work,
in small rooms.

Each piece begins with a thread and a drawing. The miniatures are embroidered by hand on cotton or silk, sometimes barely two centimeters across. Once finished, they're mounted on gold-leafed panels and sealed inside a custom-cut acrylic box.

Nothing is printed. Nothing is batched. A single work takes between two weeks and three months to finish.

Statement

Teresita Castelli

My work begins with a needle, a thread, and a memory that may or may not have happened.

I embroider miniature worlds — small scenes suspended between what I remember and what I imagine. Each piece is hand-stitched, built slowly, detail by detail, carrying the quiet weight of intimate time.

The acrylic dome that holds each work is not merely a frame. It has physical presence — weight, dimension — yet reads as light and transparent. It draws a real boundary around what lives inside, preserving these worlds intact, sheltered from dust and from forgetting.

I make things that ask to be looked at closely. Things that feel private, almost secret — as if you've stumbled upon something not meant for everyone.

Commissions

Tell me about the place it will live, the story you want held inside it, and the size. I'll propose a palette and a concept, and we go from there.

Petite · 20 × 20 cm

A piece the size of your palm

A single embroidered miniature, floated inside a small acrylic box. For the bedside table, the intimate corner, the gift that says something words can't. Two to four weeks from first conversation to delivery.

Jardín · 45 × 65 cm

A garden of nine figures

Nine hand-embroidered miniatures arranged across a gold-leaf field. A world you can spend time in — each figure a character, each placement a choice. Six to ten weeks, because a garden cannot be rushed.

Signature · 65 × 75 cm

A constellation of twelve

The studio's most-requested format. Room for twelve figures across a gold-leaf field — a constellation, a family, a memory held open. Eight to twelve weeks from first sketch to finished work.
